About Us

Why does someone start a guitar products company in the first place? This company was started in 1967 by Tiffany Rocker to allow anyone who was interested in guitar to be able to buy and sell guitars. We wanted people to have one place for all their guitar needs. In 1980 the company went global and opened a branch in Adelaide, Australia. 50% of our profits go towards helping children in third world countries.
